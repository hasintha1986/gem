Our History

Founded in 2005, Precious Gems began as a small family business with a passion for extraordinary gemstones. What started as a modest collection has grown into one of the most respected names in luxury gemstones today.

Over two decades, we've built relationships with ethical miners and suppliers across the globe, from the sapphire mines of Sri Lanka to the emerald fields of Colombia. Our dedication to quality and authenticity has earned us the trust of collectors, investors, and jewelry lovers worldwide.

Today, Precious Gems represents the pinnacle of gemstone excellence, offering rare and exquisite specimens that meet the highest standards of clarity, color, cut, and carat weight.
